# Stakeholder Management

## Purpose

Identify, analyze, and engage all stakeholders throughout the project lifecycle to ensure alignment and support.

## Stakeholder Categories

### 1. Executive Sponsors
- **Role:** Provide authority, resources, and high-level direction
- **Engagement:** Monthly reviews, major milestones, escalation path
- **Concerns:** ROI, timeline, strategic alignment
- **Example Roles:** CTO, VP of Product, CFO

### 2. Data Owners
- **Role:** Provide data access, ensure data quality, handle compliance
- **Engagement:** Data requirements, quality standards, access approvals
- **Concerns:** Data privacy, security, compliance
- **Example Roles:** Data Steward, Database Administrator, Privacy Officer

### 3. Business Analysts
- **Role:** Translate business requirements to technical specifications
- **Engagement:** Requirements definition, acceptance criteria, user feedback
- **Concerns:** Business value delivery, user adoption, ROI
- **Example Roles:** Business Analyst, Product Manager, Subject Matter Expert

### 4. Data Scientists & ML Engineers
- **Role:** Design, develop, and optimize models
- **Engagement:** Daily development, technical decisions, peer review
- **Concerns:** Technical feasibility, model performance, reproducibility
- **Example Roles:** ML Engineer, Data Scientist, Research Engineer

### 5. Data Engineers
- **Role:** Build and maintain data infrastructure and pipelines
- **Engagement:** Data pipeline design, infrastructure, scalability
- **Concerns:** Data quality, pipeline reliability, scalability
- **Example Roles:** Data Engineer, ETL Developer, Infrastructure Engineer

### 6. DevOps/Platform Engineers
- **Role:** Manage deployment infrastructure and monitoring
- **Engagement:** CI/CD setup, infrastructure provisioning, monitoring
- **Concerns:** Reliability, scalability, cost, security
- **Example Roles:** DevOps Engineer, Platform Engineer, SRE

### 7. Compliance & Governance
- **Role:** Ensure regulatory compliance and governance adherence
- **Engagement:** Compliance reviews, audit trails, documentation
- **Concerns:** Legal compliance, audit, documentation
- **Example Roles:** Compliance Officer, Legal Counsel, Audit Manager

### 8. End Users
- **Role:** Use the model outputs in decision-making
- **Engagement:** Feedback, acceptance testing, training
- **Concerns:** Usability, trust, explainability
- **Example Roles:** Risk Analyst, Loan Officer, Sales Representative

## Engagement Plan

### Communication Schedule
- **Daily:** Development team standups
- **Weekly:** Cross-functional team meetings
- **Bi-weekly:** Stakeholder progress reviews
- **Monthly:** Executive steering committee
- **Quarterly:** Full stakeholder reviews

### Decision-Making Authority

| Decision Type | Authority | Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| Technical approach | ML Lead + Data Lead | 1 week |
| Data requirements | Data Owner | 2 weeks |
| Business scope changes | Product Manager + Sponsor | 1 week |
| Deployment decision | Sponsor + DevOps Lead | 2 weeks |
| Model performance trade-offs | ML Lead + Business Analyst | 1 week |

### Escalation Path
1. Team Lead
2. Project Manager
3. Functional Manager
4. Executive Sponsor

## Conflict Resolution

### Process
1. **Identify conflict:** Clearly articulate the disagreement
2. **Gather perspectives:** Understand all viewpoints
3. **Find common ground:** What do all parties agree on?
4. **Develop options:** Generate multiple solutions
5. **Decide:** Make decision aligned with project objectives
6. **Document:** Record decision and rationale
7. **Communicate:** Inform all stakeholders

### Common Conflicts
- **Timeline vs. Quality:** Use success criteria to guide decision
- **Cost vs. Performance:** Reference project charter constraints
- **Complexity vs. Explainability:** Find middle ground with POC
- **Privacy vs. Data Access:** Follow compliance framework

## Best Practices

1. ✅ Identify stakeholders early in project
2. ✅ Engage stakeholders regularly and proactively
3. ✅ Be transparent about progress and challenges
4. ✅ Involve stakeholders in key decisions
5. ✅ Document decisions and rationale
6. ✅ Manage expectations actively
7. ✅ Celebrate wins with stakeholders
